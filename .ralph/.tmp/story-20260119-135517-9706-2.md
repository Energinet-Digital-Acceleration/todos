### US-007: Completed section with clear button
Status: in_progress
Depends on: US-006

Description:
As a user, I want completed todos grouped at bottom with option to clear.

Acceptance Criteria:
- [ ] Completed todos appear in separate section below active
- [ ] Section header: 'Completed' with count badge
- [ ] Section only visible when completed todos exist
- [ ] 'Clear completed' button appears in section header
- [ ] Clicking clear removes all completed todos with fade animation
- [ ] Completed items show muted styling (lighter text, checked box)
- [ ] Can uncheck to move back to active
- [ ] Example: 2 completed todos -> section shows 'Completed (2)'
- [ ] Example: click clear -> all completed removed, section hides
- [ ] Negative case: no completed todos -> section not rendered
